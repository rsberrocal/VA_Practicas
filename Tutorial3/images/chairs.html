The NH Nice hotel is a great base for exploring historic Nice and the French Riviera. It’s located in the eastern part of the city, a short tram ride from the center and the sea. The old town is just 15 minutes away on foot, while Acropolis conference center is around the corner. With parking nearby, the NH Nice is also the perfect base or stopover on a French Riviera tour.

Nice

Book a hotel in Nice

Nice is the most important port of access to one of the Mediterranean's most charismatic paradises: the French Riviera. Nice in itself is a city that has a special charm, from its Promenade des Anglais, its intense Mediterranean light, to its glamorous and cultural spirit, with museums such as the Chagall, Matisse, or the Massena Museum. NH Hotels also felt the intense power of attraction of this beautiful French city, where they have a 4-star hotel that offers rooms with all the commodities you need. If your plan is to visit the Riviera, its glamorous towns like Saint-Tropez or its famous beaches, Cannes, and Saint Raphael, staying in Nice is the best option, at least for the first stage of the journey. Wander through its narrow streets, go for a walk in Phoenix Park, discover curiosities like the Russian Orthodox Cathedral, or get drawn into the joy of Mardi Gras, are all details that gradually reveal the true identity of Nice. Come to the heart of the French Riviera with NH Hotels!
